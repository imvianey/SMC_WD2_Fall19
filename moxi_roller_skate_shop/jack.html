Moxi Jack

The Moxi Jack boot combines classic roller skate elements with modern design to create a high-performance skate built to withstand the stress of aggressive roller skating. The suede upper is backed throughout for additional support in all directions. Ankle padding and a soft, genuine Shearling tongue lining make the Jack as comfortable as it is strong, and a lace bar stabilizer helps prevent unwanted tongue rotation while skating.

  • Boot:Moxi Jack
  • Sizes: 4-11 Full & Half - Medium Width
  • Plate: Powerdyne Reactor Neo Aluminum
  • Toe Stop: Powerdyne Adjustable Black
  • Bearings: Kwik Zenith
  • Wheels: Moxi Trick 55mm 97A
